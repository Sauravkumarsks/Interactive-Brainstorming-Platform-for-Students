Frequently Asked Questions

What is MindSharp?

MindSharp is an interactive brainstorming platform for students to collaborate, share, and innovate ideas.

How do I create a brainstorming session?

You can start a brainstorming session by logging into your account and selecting "Start Brainstorming" from the dashboard.

Is this platform free to use?

Yes, MindSharp offers free access to its core features for all users. Additional premium tools are optional.

Can I save my ideas on MindSharp?

Yes! You can save your ideas by accessing the 'My Ideas' section after you've created them during your brainstorming sessions.

Where can I find resources for improving my brainstorming skills?

Yes can explore helpful materials and tools under the 'Resources' tab. This includes articles, guides, and tips tailored for effective idea generation.
